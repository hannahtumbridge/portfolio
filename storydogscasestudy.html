Overview

During my boot-camp experience at the University of Adelaide, one of my core projects for the project was a UI “uplift” project where UX/UI Design updates were made to a local non-for-profit. My team and I selected Story Dogs, an Australian NFP focused on improving the literacy skills of all children. Applying our design knowledge and UX research, we focused on assessing and improving the User flow and experience related to the Donation User. the key elements we wanted to focus on were:

  • Improve site navigation system
  • Implement a sitemap that works for all Users
  • Fix broken links or inconsistent User Flows
  • Build an internal payment/sign up portal
  • Improve colour accessibility
  • Build a more professional UI system
  • Ensure the uplift adhears to Responsive Web Design

    • Key Statement

    Challenge

    The significance of the work done by the team at Story Dogs is weakened by the outdated design of the current website.



    Goal

    Uplifting the current website to improve the donations user flow to a streamlined, refined experience will help Story Dogs launch more programs and collaborate with more schools across Australia.

    Design Process

    Our group approached our uplift project as a multi-step process. The main focus was to achieve a worthy update to the site within our current timeframe of 3 weeks. We broke down our process into 5 stages - Research, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Test, however our flow through these stages wasn’t necessarily linear.

    Whilst we did start with Researching the User’s that we involved in the site and performing a heuristic evaluation - we also focused on pulling together a style guide from their current social media branding or some beginning wireframes that were based off our competitor analysis.

    Working in a team meant that we could sub-divide the work across multiple fronts and move quickly through it to ensure that we met our deadline.

    Personally I enjoy the UX components of building a project and for this uplift, I focused predominantly on the Research, Define and Ideate phases and deliverables whilst assisting where I could the UI design elements such as building the style guide and providing feedback on what designs we had processed at the time.
